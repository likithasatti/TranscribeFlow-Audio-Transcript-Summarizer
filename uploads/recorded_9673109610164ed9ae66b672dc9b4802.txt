 Today's knowledge, workers and students often need to quickly review long audio content such as lectures, podcasts or meeting records listening to an entire recording is inefficient when all that's needed is the summary of the key points manually transcribing and then summarizing is a time consuming two step process that is a significant barrier to productivity. The flow aims to solve this by building a web application that takes an audio file automatically transcribed using an AI model and then generates a concise summary of the transcript.
